What is recycling?

Recycling is the process of collecting and processing materials that would otherwise be thrown away as trash and turning them into new products. Recycling can benefit your community, the economy and the environment.

Is recycling truly beneficial for the environment?

EPA data show that recycling conserves energy and natural resources. for example,Recycling just one ton of aluminum cans conserves more than 152 million Btu, the equivalent of 1,024 gallons of gasoline or 21 barrels of oil consumed.

Is recycling the best management option? What other options are there?

The most effective way to reduce waste, and the most environmentally preferred strategy, is to not create it in the first place. Source reduction, along with material reuse, are the most functional ways to save natural resources, protect the environment and save money.

What is recicla.ia?

Recicla.ia is a smart recycling bin that sorts waste at the point of disposal.

How does recicla.ia work?

The application we developed classifies the images in real time, using the integrated camera of your laptop or phone in 7 categories, which are: Cardboard, Glass, Metal, Organic, Paper, Plastic, Trash such as styrofoam plates and cups or cut paper.

Is recicla.ia a robot?

The idea is that in the future it will be an automated machine that combines robotics, AI and machine learning to divert the discarded item to its corresponding container and incentivize people to recycle with prizes through an app.
